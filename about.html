📝Know Us Better

The Sacred Heart Public School and Junior College was established in the year 1996
with the foresight of its Patron. His Grace Mar Joseph Powathil and is affiliated to the
Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. The School caters to the age group
3 years to 18 years from classes LKG to XII and to the academic needs of the people
in and around Changanacherry irrespective of their community, caste and creed. Since
its inception, it has been growing steadily and rapidly. The college insists on quality
education that develops the curricular and co-curricular talents of the students in a
disciplined environment.
